Travel Services Company Case Study

The Challenge

This travel services company had an existing affiliate program with less than 50 active partners, most of which were generating minimal bookings. They needed to significantly expand their affiliate network while ensuring new partners were high-quality and aligned with their brand values.

The Solution

We developed a comprehensive recruitment and management strategy:

  • Tiered Commission Structure: Created incentives for affiliates to increase booking volume and quality.
  • Niche Targeting: Focused on travel bloggers, review sites, and vacation planners in specific destinations.
  • Training Program: Developed resources to help affiliates promote services more effectively.
  • Regular Communication: Implemented monthly newsletters with promotional ideas and performance insights.
